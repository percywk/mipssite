About Me

I created this site because I was tired of the low quality introductions to MIPS. My first introduction was in an Architecture class I took. I quickly realized a majority of the previous general techniques were mostly worthless. MIPS is not a language that is a pleasure to work in. It is an assembly language designed to test your skills and improve you.

Currently, I am working on a MIPS program for Sudoku. It is painful. Higher level comparisons are difficult to do. They involve a difficult concept that must be split apart piece by piece taking a large amount of time in the process. For example, selecting unique possibilities among a row or column is quite challenging. The simplest way possible wins in the end. For something like uniqueness summing over possibilities was useful.
